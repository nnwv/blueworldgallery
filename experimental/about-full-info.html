About Blue World Gallery

Blue World Gallery is a digital art gallery project by Kaja Silverman.
The gallery has three to four rooms each characterized by a different chapter of metaphysics.
Gold Room: Classical Metaphysics (worship of the golden calf)
Giotto Room: The First Chapter of Modern Metaphysis (the sky becomes blue)
Dark Room: The Second Chapter of Modern Metaphysics (descartres closed eyes)
Computer Room: Third Chapter of Modern Metaphysics (technological dream of infinite space)
